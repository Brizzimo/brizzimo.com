About Brizzimo


Brizzimo Solutions is a very, very small engineering company based in the Bay Area, CA owned and operated by a single young engineer, Michele Brianti. While Michele's degree may be in mechanical engineering, he has experience in many different disciplines including electronics, robotics, sewing, coding, construction, and just about every type of manufacturing process allowing him to create and design products to fit any need.

Most products created and developed by Brizzimo have been personal projects, but recently, Brizzimo has started to take on contracting work for anyone who requests it. Anything from a small replacement dishwasher clip to an automated hidden compartment call box, we can make it for you. The two design principles that drive all solutions are simplicity and functionality. These principles are joined by a desire to be as sustainable as possible, making products through repurposing or with materials that would otherwise be scrapped. Brizzimo is basically the master of using that scrap piece of wood you keep around ~just in case~. By clicking on the solutions tag at the top of the page or by visiting the brizzimo.solutions instagram account, you will be able to get an idea for the things we can create.
